Aniket Shahi | Cybersecurity Enthusiast | Aspiring Engineer



Hey there! I'm Aniket Shahi, on an exciting journey pursuing a B.Tech-M.Tech (Integrated) degree in Computer Science and Engineering, specializing in Cybersecurity. My passion for innovation led me to achieve the "ALPHA" level in the Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors (MLSA) program, where I actively contribute to the community and showcase my growth and expertise.

As a Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) Co-Lead at National Forensic Sciences University - Delhi, I'm thrilled to lead a vibrant community of students interested in Google developer technologies. Organizing workshops and events, I aim to empower my peers and make a positive impact on the tech community.

InSIG'23 Fellow at the India School on Internet Governance, I'm diving deep into the ever-evolving Internet landscape. This experience equips me with insights into global Internet Governance, core technologies, multistakeholderism, and the challenges and opportunities presented by emerging technologies.

My journey as a Cybersecurity Intern at Bhumi iTech Pvt. Ltd. was nothing short of incredible. I delved into deploying the cutting-edge SOAR framework as part of the Blue Team, honing my skills in incident response automation and security operations management.

Certified in AWS and Google Cloud, my expertise extends to foundational infrastructure tasks and machine learning foundations. Beyond academics, I'm a passionate advocate for community service, contributing to organizations like Literacy India and the Robin Hood Army.

Let's connect and collaborate to build a secure digital landscape and foster innovation in technology. Exciting times ahead!
